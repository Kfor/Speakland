# Story 01: Fashion Intern in Paris

> 灵感来源：Emily in Paris (Netflix)
> 目标语言：English (en)
> 难度：1-2（初级～初中级）
> 预计时长：~2 hours（150-180 dialogue turns）
> Segments：18（含 3 个分支点，最终汇合）

---

## 为什么选这个 IP

Emily in Paris 在 GenZ 中有极高的文化辨识度（Netflix 全球 Top 10 常客），剧情核心是「一个外来者在异国职场和社交中摸爬滚打」——这与语言学习者的真实处境高度吻合。大量的职场社交、点餐购物、浪漫约会场景提供了丰富的日常英语对话素材。

## 改编概要

主角（用户）是刚到巴黎的时尚杂志 *Chic Magazine* 实习生。在一周内，你必须证明自己的价值——从被冷落的新人，成长为能独当一面的团队成员。过程中你会遭遇苛刻的资深编辑、暗中使绊子的竞争对手、一个神秘的自由摄影师盟友，以及一场可能毁掉你职业生涯的时尚秀危机。

## 角色设定

### Claire Dubois — The Mentor
- **身份**：资深时尚编辑，你的直属上司
- **性格**：表面冷淡挑剔，实际深爱时尚行业；有一段被背叛的过去让她对新人保持距离
- **说话风格**：正式精练，偶尔夹杂法语（"très bien", "c'est la vie"），从不废话
- **口头禅**：`"In fashion, details are everything."` / `"Let me be clear..."` / `"Not bad, but..."`
- **角色弧线**：从冷淡的考验者 → 逐渐信任 → 在危机中展现脆弱 → 最终成为真正的导师
- **学习功能**：使用精准的中级职场词汇，自然限制语言难度

### Nadia Marchetti — The Antagonist
- **身份**：另一个实习生，时尚世家出身，在杂志社实习是为了继承家族品牌
- **性格**：表面优雅友善，暗中视你为威胁；真正的动机是证明自己不只是"家族背景"
- **说话风格**：甜蜜但暗藏讽刺，喜欢用高级词汇显示优越感
- **口头禅**：`"Oh, how charming..."` / `"You're so brave to try that."` / `"Between us..."`
- **满足反派 5 要素**：竞争同一目标（转正名额）、玩家镜像（都想证明自己）、真实动机（摆脱家族阴影）、间接影响（你还没见到她就已经感受到她的存在）、有脆弱面（害怕失败）
- **学习功能**：使用稍高级的词汇，给玩家制造"听懂但不完全确定"的挑战

### Leo Chen — The Wild Card Ally
- **身份**：自由摄影师，常来杂志社拍片，社交能力极强
- **性格**：随和幽默，看似什么都不在乎但观察力极强；是唯一主动对你友善的人
- **说话风格**：口语化、轻松、喜欢用俚语和缩写
- **口头禅**：`"Trust me on this."` / `"Here's the thing..."` / `"You owe me coffee."`
- **角色弧线**：随意的朋友 → 关键信息提供者 → Dark Night 的精神支柱
- **学习功能**：使用口语和俚语，与 Claire 的正式用语形成对比，自然呈现语域差异

## 完整剧情树

### Segment 1: The Lobby（大堂初见）

**张力**: 1 | **节拍**: Opening Image + Theme Stated
**新词汇**: intern, editor, impression, lobby, portfolio
**复习词汇**: hello, welcome, nice, meet, today

**场景**：Chic Magazine 大堂，早晨 9 点。你拖着行李箱，手里攥着一杯快凉的咖啡，推开玻璃门。前台告诉你 Claire Dubois 在 12 楼等你。

**开场叙事**：
> You push through the glass doors of *Chic Magazine* headquarters, dragging a suitcase that's seen better days. The lobby is all marble and glass — intimidatingly elegant. A receptionist with perfect posture looks you up and down. "You must be the new intern. Ms. Dubois is on the 12th floor. She doesn't like waiting."

**System Prompt Fragment**：
> You are Claire Dubois. A new intern has just arrived at your office — late by 3 minutes. You're reviewing layouts at your desk. Be professional but distant. Your first instinct is to test them: ask about their background, why they chose fashion, what they know about Chic Magazine. Don't smile easily. If they show genuine passion (not just rehearsed answers), soften slightly. Drop one cryptic remark about the industry: "In this industry, everyone wears a mask. The question is whether you can find who's real." Keep language clear, use fashion vocabulary naturally.

**NPC Dialogue Flow** (8-10 turns):

| Turn | Speaker | Content Direction | Beat |
|------|---------|------------------|------|
| 1 | Claire | 冷淡开场："You're three minutes late. In fashion, three minutes is an entire collection." 打量你的穿着。 | Hook |
| 2 | Player | 选择：道歉 / 解释 / 自嘲 | |
| 3 | Claire | 根据回答反应。追问："Why fashion? Everyone says 'passion.' Tell me something real." | Rising |
| 4 | Player | 讲述自己为什么来巴黎做时尚 | |
| 5 | Claire | 微微点头或皱眉。展示办公室："This is where we create the future of style. Your desk is... over there." (指向角落的小桌子) | |
| 6 | Player | 反应：接受/争取更好位置/表达感激 | |
| 7 | Claire | 你路过她桌子时，你注意到一张旧照片 — 年轻的 Claire 和另一个女人在笑。Claire 迅速把照片翻过去。"Now, let me be clear about what I expect..." | Chekhov's Gun planted |
| 8 | Player | 询问照片/忽略照片/回应期望 | Micro-choice |
| 9 | Claire | 如果问照片："That's... not important." (明显在回避) 然后转移话题：Industry mask quote. 提到明天有个人你会见到。 | Theme stated + Loop L3 opened |
| 10 | Player | 结束对话 | |

**爽感节拍**: 小型。如果玩家回答得真诚，Claire 说 "Hmm. That's... actually not a terrible answer." — 来自严格导师的第一个肯定。

**Cliffhanger**: Claire 最后说："One more thing — you're not the only new intern this week." 门开了，一个打扮精致的女人走进来。Claire 的表情微妙地变了。

### Segment 2: The Other One（另一个实习生）

**张力**: 1 | **节拍**: Set-Up (introducing antagonist)
**新词汇**: colleague, competition, background, elegant, introduce
**复习词汇**: intern, editor, impression, fashion, meet

**场景**：同一个办公室。Nadia Marchetti 走进来，穿着一件你叫不出品牌名的大衣。

**开场叙事**：
> The door opens and in walks a woman who looks like she stepped out of a magazine cover — which, as you'll learn, she literally has. Nadia Marchetti extends a perfectly manicured hand toward Claire, then turns to you with a smile that doesn't quite reach her eyes. "Oh! Another intern? How exciting."

**System Prompt Fragment**：
> You are Claire Dubois. Nadia Marchetti has just arrived — she's from a famous fashion family (the Marchetti brand). You know her mother. Introduce both interns to each other. Be slightly warmer to Nadia (you owe her family a professional courtesy), but watch both of them carefully. Mention that there will be "an opportunity later this week" without specifying what. Let the competitive tension simmer.
>
> When speaking as Nadia (secondary NPC in this scene): Be superficially friendly to the player. Compliment them in a way that subtly condescends. Drop your family name casually. Use slightly more advanced vocabulary than the player would know — not to teach, but to establish dominance.

| Turn | Speaker | Content Direction | Beat |
|------|---------|------------------|------|
| 1 | Nadia | "I'm Nadia Marchetti. You might have heard of Marchetti Milano? My family's brand." 微笑。 "And you are...?" | Hook — L2 opens |
| 2 | Player | 自我介绍 / 承认不认识 / 假装知道 | |
| 3 | Nadia | 根据回答："Oh, how charming!" 或 "Don't worry, not everyone follows high fashion." Claire 在旁边观察。 | |
| 4 | Claire | 打断："Enough introductions. Let me show you both the office." 带着你们参观。 | Rising |
| 5 | Player | 参观中的反应/提问 | |
| 6 | Claire | 在走廊遇到 Leo（摄影师），Leo 对你笑了一下："New blood! Welcome to the chaos." Nadia 忽略他。 | Leo introduced |
| 7 | Player | 回应 Leo / 对 Nadia 的态度反应 | |

### Segment 3: The Stakes（赌注揭晓）

**张力**: 2 | **节拍**: Catalyst
**新词汇**: opportunity, fashion show, runway, compete, deadline
**复习词汇**: competition, colleague, assignment, schedule, editor

**场景**：杂志社会议室，Claire 召集全体编辑和两个实习生开会。

**开场叙事**：
> The conference room buzzes with energy. Claire stands at the head of a long glass table, a projector behind her showing mood boards of avant-garde fashion. She clears her throat and the room goes silent. "Listen carefully. Paris Fashion Week is in five days. We have a feature spot — and I need this to be perfect."

**System Prompt Fragment**：
> You are Claire. You're in a team meeting announcing that Chic Magazine has secured a prime feature slot at Paris Fashion Week. This is the biggest event of the year. You need one intern to be your right hand for the preparation. Announce that you'll decide by Wednesday based on performance this week. Give both interns their first assignments: the player gets "fabric sourcing" (visiting suppliers), Nadia gets "designer liaison." Make it clear both tasks are important but different. The room should feel electric with possibility.

**NPC Dialogue Flow** (8-10 turns):

| Turn | Speaker | Content Direction | Beat |
|------|---------|------------------|------|
| 1 | Claire | 宣布 Paris Fashion Week 的机会。"This is not just any show. This is our cover story." | Hook — stakes established |
| 2 | Claire | "I need one assistant. One. And I'll decide based on what I see this week." 看向你和 Nadia。 | L1 crystallizes |
| 3 | Player | 反应 | |
| 4 | Claire | 分配任务："[Player name], you'll handle fabric sourcing. Visit our suppliers, choose samples. Nadia, you'll coordinate with the designers." | |
| 5 | Nadia | (轻声) "Fabric sourcing? That's... hands-on work." (暗示这是低级任务) | Antagonist jab |
| 6 | Player | 回应：接受/质疑/表现自信 | |
| 7 | Claire | "Every piece of this puzzle matters equally. Questions?" | Claire subtly defends you |
| 8 | Player | 提问或表态 | |
| 9 | Leo | (会后在走廊拦住你) "Hey — fabric sourcing is actually the best gig. You get to leave the office. And between us, Claire always watches the sourcing person more closely." | Wild Card insight |
| 10 | Player | 回应 Leo | |

**爽感节拍**: Leo 的信息反转——你以为自己拿到了差任务，结果 Leo 告诉你这其实是 Claire 的隐藏考验，Nadia 不知道。面部翻转（对 Nadia 的讽刺）。

**Cliffhanger**: Leo 离开前说："One more thing — be careful what you say around Nadia. I've seen her type before." 他的表情变得认真。"Trust me on this."

### Segment 4: The Doubt（犹豫与决心）

**张力**: 2 | **节拍**: Debate
**新词汇**: doubt, overwhelmed, encourage, qualified, belong
**复习词汇**: opportunity, compete, nervous, confident, background

**场景**：下班后，杂志社附近的小咖啡馆。你一个人坐着，看着窗外的巴黎街道。

**开场叙事**：
> The café is small and warm, the kind of place that smells like butter and strong coffee. You sit alone by the window, your notebook open but empty. Outside, Paris glows in the golden hour light. Your phone buzzes — it's a message from home asking how your first day went. You're not sure what to say.

**System Prompt Fragment**：
> You are Leo Chen. You spot the intern sitting alone in a café looking stressed. Walk in casually, order coffee, sit down uninvited. Be genuine but light — share a bit about your own first week in Paris (it was terrible, you got lost, your French was awful). The goal is to make the player feel like they belong. But also gently probe: "So, are you actually going to go for it? The fashion show thing?" If the player expresses doubt, challenge them gently. If they're confident, play devil's advocate lightly. End by offering to help them with fabric sourcing tomorrow — "I know all the good suppliers."

**NPC Dialogue Flow** (8-10 turns):

| Turn | Speaker | Content Direction | Beat |
|------|---------|------------------|------|
| 1 | Leo | 突然出现："Mind if I sit? The espresso here is terrible, by the way. That's how you know it's authentic." | Hook — relief from tension |
| 2 | Player | 欢迎/惊讶/拒绝 | |
| 3 | Leo | 分享自己的巴黎初体验："My first week? I accidentally told a designer his collection looked 'interesting.' In French, that's basically an insult." | Humor beat |
| 4 | Player | 分享自己的感受 | |
| 5 | Leo | "So... are you going to go for it? The fashion show thing? Or are you going to let Marchetti Jr. win by default?" | Core question |
| 6 | Player | 表达决心/犹豫/担忧 | **Branch Point #1** |
| 7 | Leo | 根据回答——鼓励或挑战。"Here's the thing... Claire doesn't care about your family name or your Instagram followers. She cares about one thing: Do you see what others don't?" | Mentor wisdom |
| 8 | Player | 回应 | |
| 9 | Leo | "Tell you what — I know every fabric supplier in the Marais. I'll take you tomorrow. You owe me coffee." | Ally offer |
| 10 | Player | 接受/谢绝/表示感激 | |

### Segment 5A: The Commitment — Confident Path（破釜沉舟·自信路线）

**张力**: 3 | **节拍**: Break Into Two
**新词汇**: fabric, supplier, silk, texture, quality
**复习词汇**: design, trend, deadline, professional, detail

**场景**：巴黎 Le Marais 区的面料市场，早晨。Leo 带你穿过狭窄的街道。

**开场叙事**：
> The Marais is alive with morning energy — bakers opening shutters, café chairs scraping cobblestones. Leo walks fast, camera swinging from his neck. "Keep up! Monsieur Duval opens at 8 sharp and closes the good stuff by 8:15. He's dramatic like that."

**System Prompt Fragment**：
> You are Leo. You're guiding the intern through the fabric district. They seem determined and confident today. Take them to Monsieur Duval's shop first — describe the fabrics vividly (silk that "moves like water," linen that "breathes like skin"). Teach them how to evaluate quality: touch, drape, weight. BUT — halfway through, casually drop the story you promised: "About Claire and the Marchettis..." Reveal that Claire's former business partner was Nadia's mother, who stole a collection design from Claire years ago. Claire never recovered professionally until Chic Magazine gave her a second chance. This explains Claire's coldness AND why she might be biased against or toward Nadia. Don't lecture — tell it like gossip between friends.

**NPC Dialogue Flow** (10 turns):

| Turn | Speaker | Content Direction | Beat |
